How to play

The gameplay of Earth Defence Force: Elite Asteroid Division is fairly straightforward.


    1. Click the start button
    2. Select your difficulty
    3. Aim with the mouse
    4. Click to fire a missile at your mouse location
    5. Shoot down the meteors before all the cities are destroyed
